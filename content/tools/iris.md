Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. Iris puts a live countdown in your menu bar (`👁 14:23`) and plays an audio cue when it's time. Pause or snooze from the menu. Starts automatically on login via a LaunchAgent.

Actually use this one.
