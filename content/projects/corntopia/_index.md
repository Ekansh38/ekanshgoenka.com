me and my friends started a fake digital "country" at school. It first began as paper-based fake country, with money tracking, turned into a full Django web app: a banking system, a crypto exchange with real order matching, a marketplace where students bought and sold stuff, and a chat system.

peaked at 56 users and 64 active listings before a student tipped off the teacher. I was called in, accused of fraud, and panicked-deleted the entire server and database on the spot.

full story + screenshots on GitHub.
