The language of text editing. Once you learn the grammar — verbs (`d`elete, `c`hange, `y`ank) combined with nouns (`w`ord, `i`nner `"`, `a` `p`aragraph) — you stop thinking about keystrokes and start thinking about intent. Available everywhere: Neovim, VS Code, JetBrains, even your browser.
